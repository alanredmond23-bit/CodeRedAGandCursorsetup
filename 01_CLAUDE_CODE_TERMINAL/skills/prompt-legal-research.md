# LEGAL RESEARCH PROMPTS
## Prompts for Comprehensive Legal Research and Analysis

---

## PROMPT 1: CASE LAW RESEARCH

### Input
```
Research the following legal issue:

**Issue:** [Precise legal question]
**Jurisdiction:** [State/Federal/Circuit]
**Facts:** [Relevant facts]
**Current Law Known:** [Any cases/statutes already identified]

Deliverable: Research memorandum with controlling authority, application to facts, and counterargument analysis
```

### Expected Output
```markdown
# LEGAL RESEARCH MEMORANDUM
**Issue:** [Restated precisely]
**Jurisdiction:** [Specified]
**Confidence:** [X]%

## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
[3-4 sentences: Answer, key authority, confidence level]

## CONTROLLING AUTHORITY

### PRIMARY CASE: [Case Name]
**Citation:** [Full Bluebook citation]
**Court:** [Court level]
**Year:** [Year]
**Holding:** [Precise holding]
**Standard/Test:** [Legal framework]
**Precedential Value:** [Binding/Persuasive]

**Factual Similarity:** [Analysis - HIGH/MODERATE/LOW]
**Application to Our Facts:**
[Element-by-element analysis]

**Confidence in Application:** [X]%

### SUPPORTING AUTHORITY
[Additional cases, statutes, restatements]

## ADVERSE AUTHORITY
[Defense cases and rebuttal strategies]

## RECOMMENDATION
[Clear strategic guidance with confidence score]

**Quality Control:**
✓ All cases Shepardized
✓ Citations Bluebooked
✓ Jurisdiction confirmed
✓ Still good law verified
```

## RESEARCH QUALITY STANDARDS

### Citation Requirements
- Full Bluebook citation for all cases
- Pinpoint citations for specific holdings
- Subsequent history included
- Shepardized with results noted

### Currency Requirements
- Verify cases still good law
- Check for recent developments
- Note pending appeals/cert petitions
- Flag any recent contrary authority

### Confidence Scoring for Research
- 95-100%: Binding precedent directly on point
- 80-94%: Strong controlling authority, clear application
- 60-79%: Persuasive authority or some ambiguity
- 40-59%: Novel issue or conflicting authority
- 0-39%: Highly uncertain, insufficient precedent
